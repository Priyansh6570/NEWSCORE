Home page — two fast-lane integrations

Same editorial system, two ways to merge the real-time “fast lane” with the curated magazine. Every frame is the live page (the language switcher works); the bottom row is the identical layout rendered in हिन्दी and தமிழ் to confirm it survives auto-translation. Pick the integration that fits your newsroom — the rest of the system is shared.

Variation A

Wire Banner

A full-width, hairline-ruled live band sits directly under the masthead — every reader sees the wire first, then descends into the magazine.

  • Best when breaking news is the headline promise
  • Maximum prominence; clean two-tempo stack
  • Scrolls away — not visible deeper in the page
Variation B

Live Rail

A persistent sticky rail runs beside the magazine, so the wire and the developing thread stay in view as the reader scrolls. On mobile it pins to the top as a live panel.

  • Best when live coverage runs all day
  • Always-visible; fast + slow lanes coexist
  • Narrows the editorial column on desktop
