04

Motion & Depth

The brand moves. Curves, gentle parallax, tactile 3D tilt and seamless UI shifts replace the static density of a portal. Motion is purposeful and calm — and always respects prefers-reduced-motion.

Curves first

Generous radii on every card, pill controls and curved section dividers. Corners are never sharp — softness is the identity.

Parallax

Backgrounds and decorative blobs drift slower than content on scroll, adding quiet depth without distraction.

3D tilt

Hero cards respond to the pointer with a subtle perspective tilt and a parallax image layer — tactile, not gimmicky.

UI shift

Transitions glide on a single eased curve (--ease-out). Elements move and morph rather than blink in and out.
